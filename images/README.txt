IMAGES & MEDIA — Put all your photos and videos here
=====================================================

Use this single folder for both panel photos and videos. Drop your files into the subfolders below.

PANEL COMPARISON PHOTOS
  images/panels/
    • old.jpg (or old.png) — Traditional/blue panels photo
    • new.jpg (or new.png) — Modern all-black panels photo
  These appear in the Panel Aesthetics Comparison and the before/after slider.

INSTALLATION VIDEOS
  images/installation/
    • install.mp4 — Installation process (timelapse)
    • final.mp4  — Final look (drone or walkthrough)
  Supported: MP4, WebM

CUSTOMER TESTIMONIAL VIDEOS
  images/testimonials/
    • customer1.mp4 — First testimonial
    • customer2.mp4 — Second testimonial
    • customer3.mp4 — Third testimonial
  Supported: MP4, WebM

Keep index.html in the same folder as this "images" folder (e.g. Solar financial T/).
You can still use "Use your photo" in the app to pick images from your device during a pitch.
